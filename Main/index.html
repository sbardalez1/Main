About Wolf Grave Games

Wanna Know About Wolf Grave Games?

We are a group of gamers who wanted to turn our love of gaming and nerd culture/community into a career. We plan to offer tournaments for wargaming, trading card games, fighting games, and more.

Services

Terrain Rental

From wargame hills and trees to cozy inns and full dungeons, we offer professionally hand-painted terrain for any game you're playing. Who wants a full castle in the house if you'll only use it once or twice a year? Use what you need, then bring it back for the next scene!

Friday Night Magic

A time-honored tradition of getting way too amped-up about cardboard. Control, aggro, and midrange your way to the top every friday night.

Dungeons and Dragons

Pretend to be an elf princess for a few hours a week with us! Our DMs collaborate weekly to give our players heart-pounding action and gut-wrenching tragedy. Player spots are available with membership or apply to be a DM if that's your preference, too!

Pokemon TCG

Pikachu, Eevee, and Charizard battle it out every Thursday for fabulous prizes!

Fighting game tournaments

Smash, Tekken, Soul Calibur, and more! Saturday nights from 8-12
